Privacy Policy for ScanDoc Pro

Last Updated: January 2025

ScanDoc Pro ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our app handles your data. By using ScanDoc Pro, you agree to the terms described in this policy.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to make it very clear that ScanDoc Pro does NOT collect or store:

  • Personal information
  • Images or documents
  • PDF files, extracted text, or scanning data
  • Location data
  • Contacts, messages, or call logs

All data stays on your device only.

2. Images, PDF & Text Processing

All processing (Image to Text, PDF to Text, Multi-Image PDF creation) happens locally on your device. No image, text, or file is uploaded to any server.

3. Storage Permissions

Our app requires certain permissions to function properly:

  • Camera: Used only for capturing images for scanning.
  • Storage / Photos: Used only to select and save images/PDFs.

We do not access or collect any other files unrelated to the app.

4. Third-Party Services (Ads)

ScanDoc Pro uses Google AdMob for advertising. AdMob may collect limited, non-personal data for showing relevant ads.

For more details, refer to Google’s Privacy Policy:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

5. Children’s Privacy

Our app does not target children. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

6. Data Security

All your documents are stored locally on your device. We never upload or share them. You have full control to delete your files anytime.
